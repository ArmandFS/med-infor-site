Medical Informatics Courses

Medical Database & Optimization

This course focuses on the management and optimization of medical databases. Students will learn about data modeling, query optimization, and database security.

Tech Stack: SQL, Database Management Systems, Data Modeling. VMs, Linux OS, & PHP

Medical Information Management

This course covers the management of medical information systems. Students will explore electronic health records (EHRs), and utilize linux to operate on the BME Servers

Tech Stack: Health IT Standards, EHR Systems, Healthcare Informatics. Linux OS, C Programming Language.

Genomic Computation

In this course, students dive into genomic data analysis and computation. Topics include DNA sequencing, genomic databases, and bioinformatics tools.

Tech Stack: Python Libraries such as NumPy and Matplotlib can help visualize genomic structures, Genomic Databases offer a wide range of data.
